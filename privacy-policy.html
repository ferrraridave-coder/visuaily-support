Privacy Policy for VisuAlly

Last updated: 2 December 2025

VisuAlly is developed by David Picknell (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when using the VisuAlly app. If you have questions, you can contact us at: ferrraridave@icloud.com.

1. Information You Provide

VisuAlly allows you to upload images, reference photos, and text prompts to generate AI-created images and videos. This content is securely sent to Replicate, our third-party AI inference provider, for processing.

2. How Replicate Handles Your Data

Replicate temporarily stores your uploaded inputs and generated outputs for approximately one hour, after which they are automatically deleted. Details of Replicate’s retention policy: https://replicate.com/docs/topics/predictions/data-retention.

3. What We Store

We do not store your input images, reference photos, prompts, or generated media. Only minimal usage data is stored in Apple CloudKit, such as:

  • Your Apple ID’s CloudKit anonymous identifier (not your actual Apple ID)
  • Your active subscription tier
  • Your remaining generation counts

No personal images or prompts are ever stored by us.

4. Sharing of Data

Your content is shared only with Replicate for processing. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties.

5. Children

VisuAlly is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of all CloudKit-stored usage data by contacting us at the email above.
