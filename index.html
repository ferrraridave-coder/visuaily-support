Frequently Asked Questions

What does VisuAlly do?

VisuAlly allows you to generate AI images and videos using a curated selection of advanced AI models. You can upload photos, reference images, and text prompts to create or edit visual content.

Do I need an account?

No additional account is required. Your subscription is handled through your Apple ID, and all AI processing is performed anonymously by Replicate.

How is my data handled?

Uploaded images, reference photos, and prompts are securely sent to Replicate for AI processing. Replicate temporarily stores your content for approximately one hour before automatic deletion. We do not store or retain your content.

How do I report a bug?

Email us with a description of the issue and—if possible—a screenshot. We will reply as soon as possible.
