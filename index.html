Frequently Asked Questions

What does VisuAlly do?
VisuAlly allows you to generate AI images and videos using a curated selection of advanced AI models. You can upload photos, reference images, and text prompts to create or edit visual content.

Do I need an account?
No additional account is required. Your subscription is handled through your Apple ID, and all processing is performed anonymously by Replicate.

How is my data handled?
Uploaded images, reference photos, and generated outputs are temporarily processed by Replicate. They delete this data automatically after around one hour. We do not store your images or prompts.

Why did my generation fail?
Some models can be sensitive to image size or prompt structure. If a generation fails, try reducing the image resolution or simplifying the prompt. If the issue continues, contact support.

What models are supported?
VisuAlly includes over 80+ AI models for image creation, editing, upscaling, inpainting, face editing, and video generation. The model list may evolve over time as we adopt newer and better models.

Is there a subscription?
Yes. VisuAlly offers subscription tiers for image and video generation. Each tier includes a monthly allowance of generations and access to all supported models.

How do I report a bug or issue?
Please email a description of the problem and attach a screenshot if possible. You will receive a reply as soon as possible.
